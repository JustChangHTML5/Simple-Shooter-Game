Controls: W, A, S, D movement (A little wonky because there is only one way to do this. (Don't hold down the keys)), click to shoot, and press "v" to pause. There are four modes that ascend in difficulty, Easy Mode, Normal Mode, Hard Mode, and Extreme Mode. In Easy Mode and Hard Mode, you can shoot multiple bullets at the enemies by pressing the key, "f". In Hard Mode and Extreme Mode, the enemies dodge you bullets. You can click on the button that says No special attack to switch beetween special attacks, right click to activate them (They all have different cooldown times). The cheats button makes the cooldowns for the special attacks 0 (Cannot be reset unless you refresh the game). The mute button mutes all sound. If there are any problems, please tell me. Anyways, Enjoy!
